Our Wordpress plug-in lets you easily configure your Wordpress site to use your Spinnakr account.

== Description ==

Quickly and easily add Spinnakr to your Wordpress site with no coding experience required.  Setup is as simple as visiting the Spinnakr Settings page in your Wordpress administration panel and following the instructions. If you don't have a Spinnakr account yet, signup for one at <a href="http://spinnakr.com/">http://spinnakr.com</a>.
